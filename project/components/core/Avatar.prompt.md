**Avatar** — round image avatar for shelters, providers and users, with a warm initials fallback.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Mancs Menhely" size={56} ring />
<Avatar src="/shelter.jpg" alt="Cicaház" size={40} />
```

Props: `src`, `name`/`alt` (initials fallback), `size` (px diameter), `ring` (camel ring).
